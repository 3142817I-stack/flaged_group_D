About Us

FlagD is an interactive, engaging and dynamic flag guessing website.

Built for everyone, whether you're a student looking to brush up on their flag knowledge for a geography quiz, a teacher looking for an interactive way to teach students, maybe you're someone who wants to show off their general knowledge or compete against others in our timed leaderboards - whatever it is you're looking for we've got you covered!

FlagD has been designed to allow users (yes YOU reading this) to learn flags from memory and recognize countries across the world from each continent so that you can stay engaged while studying and ace that upcoming test!
